A man named "Big Joe Mufferaw" creates a Flat Earth Society group in Canada. He tries to enlighten people on how and why the Earth is flat. He gains a lot of support from people across the world, but many people disagree with his claims and tries to use violence to go against him and his supporters, which causes an intense worldwide war between the Flat Earthers and those that don't believe the Earth is flat.
